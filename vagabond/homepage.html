The Philosophy of Musashi in Vagabond.

Vagabond is one of the best-selling manga of all time, often regarded as a masterpiece to many. At first glance, a story set in samurai-period Japan with a protagonist inspired by the legendary Miyamoto Musashi may not seem like anything out of the ordinary - but the philosophical themes and unique introspective storytelling makes it like none other, resonating deeply with fans across the world.

Introduction

Who was Miyamoto Musashi?

Miyamoto Musashi is widely considered to have been one of the most skillful samurai in all of history, referred to as Japan's "sword saint". By the end of his natural life, he had created a unique technique of swordsmanship and wrote various philosophical works that are still studied today.

What is Vagabond?

Vagabond is a historical-fiction manga series written and illustrated by Takehiko Inoue. It follows the journey of Miyamoto Musashi.
